# Electric Vehicles Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes Electric Vehicle (EV) registration data to uncover trends in vehicle adoption, manufacturer performance, model popularity, and geographic distribution across states.

The project follows a complete data analytics workflow:

* Data Cleaning and Preprocessing using Python (Pandas)
* Data Exploration and Validation
* Interactive Dashboard Development using Tableau
* Business Insights and Recommendations

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Objectives

The main objectives of this project are:

* Analyze the growth of electric vehicles over time.
* Identify the most popular EV manufacturers and models.
* Examine the distribution of EVs across states.
* Evaluate CAFV (Clean Alternative Fuel Vehicle) eligibility categories.
* Measure the average electric range of registered vehicles.

## Dashboard KPIs

### Total Vehicles

Displays the total number of electric vehicles in the dataset.

### Average Electric Range

Shows the average driving range of EVs.

### Top 10 Vehicles by Make

Highlights the most popular manufacturers.

### Top 10 Vehicles by Model

Shows the highest registered EV models.

### Vehicles by CAFV Type

Analyzes vehicle eligibility categories.

### Vehicles by Model Year

Tracks EV adoption trends over time.

### Vehicles by State

Visualizes geographic distribution across states.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Key Insights

* Tesla dominates EV registrations among manufacturers.
* Model S and Model 3 are among the most registered vehicle models.
* EV adoption has increased significantly in recent years.
* Vehicle registrations are concentrated in a limited number of states.
* CAFV-eligible vehicles represent a major portion of the dataset.
* The average electric range indicates continuous improvement in EV technology.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Recommendations

### 1. Expand Charging Infrastructure

States with high EV adoption should continue investing in charging networks to support future growth.

### 2. Promote EV Adoption in Low-Registration States

Governments and manufacturers should target regions with lower adoption rates through incentives and awareness campaigns.

### 3. Support Long-Range EV Development

Manufacturers should focus on increasing electric range to improve consumer confidence and reduce range anxiety.

### 4. Monitor Emerging Manufacturers

While Tesla currently leads the market, tracking growth among other manufacturers can reveal future opportunities.

### 5. Incentivize CAFV-Eligible Vehicles

Encouraging CAFV-eligible vehicles can accelerate environmental sustainability goals.
